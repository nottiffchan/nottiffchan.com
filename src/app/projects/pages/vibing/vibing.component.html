How it works

User authenticates their Spotify account, then enters the names of the individuals that the playlist is being created for

Both individuals enter 2 songs each that encapsulate the vibe of music they like

Algorithm generates a ready-to-play Spotify playlist based on the 4 songs inputted

Reflection

I’m really proud of this app. Although it’s humble and has a rather simple concept, to me it represents a milestone that I realised I had reached as a developer. During the hackathon itself, as a team of experienced newbies, we were unable to figure out the Spotify authentication process and how to call the API functions within the short timeline of 24 hours, and tragically could not submit a completed product at the end of the hackathon.

2 months later however, with a renewed sense of purpose and also 2 months into my first dev internship, I decided to tackle the app again and complete it this time. However, I found that the front-end code I did from 2 months prior, with zero front-end experience, was terrible and I redid the whole app from scratch. The moment I successfully deployed the web app on Heroku and listened to the first playlist it generated affirmed to me that I had come really far and learnt so much in the span of just a few months, and for that reason this particular project makes my heart warm.
